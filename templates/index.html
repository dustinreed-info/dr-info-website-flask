About Me:

As a Sr. DevOps Engineer with over a decade of experience in enterprise IT, I have a passion for computing that began at a young age. From creating my first application in QBasic at age 8 to developing text-based games on my TI-83 calculator in high school, I have always been drawn to programming. My background in IT Operations has allowed me to leverage my programming knowledge to automate daily tasks, starting with batch scripts and eventually mastering PowerShell and Python.

Today, I am focused on utilizing DevOps toolchains and methodologies to design infrastructure as code and track changes with version control. By implementing CI/CD pipelines, I can deploy applications to cloud platforms in a fault-tolerant, scalable, and distributed manner. This approach reduces risk and complexity for software deployments, enabling businesses to quickly adapt to changing market conditions and be more agile.
